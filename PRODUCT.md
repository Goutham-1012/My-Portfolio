# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, AI engineering leaders, and collaborators evaluating Goutham Reddy Gunnala for senior AI, generative AI, LLMOps, and production ML roles. They arrive with limited time, scan for credibility quickly, and need proof that the portfolio's craft matches the technical claims.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio presents Goutham as a production-focused Senior AI Engineer who turns agentic AI, multimodal systems, RAG, data engineering, and LLMOps into measurable business outcomes. Success means visitors remember the nautical AI voyage, trust the production evidence, and quickly reach the resume, projects, experience, and contact paths.

## Brand Personality

Skilled, adventurous, and production-hardened. The interface should feel like a bright pirate sea chart brought to life: playful and cinematic, but still legible, credible, and clearly engineered.

## Anti-references

Do not make the site feel like a generic centered portfolio template, a plain SaaS landing page, a boxed presentation deck, or a flat 2D cartoon. Avoid changing the Sunny Corsair nautical theme, replacing the parchment/wood/ocean materials, or introducing unrelated palettes.

## Design Principles

- Preserve the voyage: every enhancement should feel like part of the same living sea chart.
- Show technical depth physically: use dimensional maps, artifacts, parallax, and motion to make complex AI work feel tangible.
- Keep proof scannable: metrics, employers, and project outcomes must remain readable at speed.
- Make motion purposeful: animation should guide attention, improve depth, or reward interaction, with reduced-motion fallbacks.
- Blend sections into the page: prefer full-width environmental layers and connected scenery over isolated boxes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text, visible focus states for controls, semantic sections and dialogs, keyboard-accessible navigation, and reduced-motion alternatives for all decorative or scroll-triggered motion.
